You Lead Coaching — Style Guide (Quick Reference)
Colours
* Gold: #C6A75E → Buttons, accents, highlights
* Deep Green: #1F3A2E → Key sections, footer
* Beige: #F4EFE7 → Background sections
* White: #FFFFFF → Main canvas
* Charcoal: #2C2C2C → Text
________________


Usage Rules
* Beige / White: 60–70%
* Green: 15–20%
* Gold: 8–12% (accents only)
👉 Gold is NOT used as a background colour
________________


Typography
Headings: Serif (Playfair Display / Cormorant)
Body: Sans-serif (Inter / Avenir)
________________


Tone
* Warm, grounded, calm authority
* Clear, not fluffy
* Emotionally intelligent
* Human, not corporate
________________


Layout Principles
* Large spacing (100px+ sections)
* Narrow text width (600–720px)
* Minimal design
* No clutter
________________


Buttons
Primary:
* Gold background
* Dark text
Secondary:
* Outline style
________________


Imagery
* Natural light
* Calm, confident presence
* No busy backgrounds
* No generic stock photos
________________
