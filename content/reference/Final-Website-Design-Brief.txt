Final Website Design Brief
________________


1. BRAND POSITIONING
________________


Core message:
Clarity → Alignment → Confidence → Sustainable Growth
Primary audience:
Driven professionals, business owners, and leaders (30–45) who:
* Carry high responsibility
* Feel mentally stretched
* Want clarity, structure, and aligned growth
Brand tone:
* Warm
* Grounded
* Calm authority
* Human, not corporate
* Strategic, not fluffy
________________


2. COLOUR SYSTEM (FINAL)
Usage Ratios (Visual Balance)
* Sand / Cream / White: 60–70%
* Deep Forest Green: 15–20%
* Gold: 8–12% (accents only)
* Charcoal: remainder
________________


Colour Palette
* Gold: #C6A75E
* Deep Green: #1F3A2E
* Beige: #F4EFE7
* White: #FFFFFF
* Optional Sage: #DCE3D9
* Charcoal text: #2C2C2C
________________


Key Rule
Gold is for accents only. Not backgrounds.
Use gold for:
* Buttons
* Underlines
* Highlights
* Small icons
________________


3. TYPOGRAPHY
Headings (Serif)
* Playfair Display OR Cormorant Garamond
* Elegant, spacious
Body (Sans-serif)
* Inter / Avenir / Helvetica Neue
Style Rules
* Generous spacing
* No dense paragraphs
* Strong hierarchy
________________


4. GLOBAL DESIGN PRINCIPLES
* Clean, minimal layout
* Large spacing (100–140px section padding)
* Narrow text blocks (600–720px max)
* No clutter
* No heavy animation
* Soft fade-in only
________________


5. HEADER / NAV BAR
