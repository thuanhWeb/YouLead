## 4) Style direction for CEO (so it matches your references)


- Keep lots of whitespace (Bloom feel)
- Soft neutral background, high-contrast text
- One accent color only
- Clean sans-serif font pairing
- Short sections, clear headings, no clutter
- Buttons consistent across page
- Use real photography (warm, grounded, professional)
- Avoid heavy animations
