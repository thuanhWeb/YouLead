3) Lead Magnet Placement Strategy (Best Fit)
Primary lead magnet
Free Mini Profiler
Place on:
* Homepage hero (primary/secondary CTA placement depending on page focus)
* Mid-page and final CTA
* Profiling page top section
Reason: strongest alignment to your method and natural bridge to clarity call/full profiler.
Secondary free magnets
5-Step Leadership Reset
Place on:
* Exit intent popup / sticky footer
* Blog posts
* Resources page
* Thank-you page after mini profiler opt-in
Boundaries Blueprint
Place on:
* Lead From Within™ page
* Burnout/overwhelm-related content
* Email nurture sequence (email #2 or #3)
Paid lead magnet
Self-Leadership Workbook (Paid)
Place on:
* Resources page
* Post-opt-in thank-you page (tripwire option)
* Email nurture soft pitch
Avoid as primary homepage CTA — keep homepage focused on free mini profiler and clarity call.

4) CTA Hierarchy Rules
Use one primary CTA per page:
* Home: Book a Clarity Call (with Mini Profiler as clear secondary)
* Mini Profiler page: Take Mini Profiler
* Coaching pages: Book Discovery/Clarity Call
* Workshops page: Enquire About Workshops
* Resources page: Download / Buy resource
Keep secondary CTA subtle and supportive.
